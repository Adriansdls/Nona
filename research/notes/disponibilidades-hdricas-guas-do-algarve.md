summary: 'Águas do Algarve official real-time reservoir storage data (updated weekly).
  Three main dams for public supply in Algarve: (1) Barragem de Odelouca: NPA 102m,
  NMC 102.35m, total capacity 157.14 hm³, useful 128.2 hm³. As of 08/05/26: 138.56
  hm³ total (88.18%), 109.62 hm³ useful (85.51%). (2) Barragem de Odeleite: NPA 52m,
  NMC 55.16m, total capacity 130 hm³, useful 108.80 hm³. As of 08/05/26: 125.36 hm³
  total (96.43%), 104.16 hm³ useful (95.74%). (3) Barragem de Beliche: NPA 52m, NMC
  53.94m, total capacity 48 hm³, useful 42.89 hm³. As of 08/05/26: 44.04 hm³ total
  (91.74%), 38.93 hm³ useful (90.76%). ALL THREE DAMS NEAR CAPACITY in May 2026 —
  stark contrast to December 2023 crisis levels. Also mentions desalination plant
  project under construction for Algarve.'
