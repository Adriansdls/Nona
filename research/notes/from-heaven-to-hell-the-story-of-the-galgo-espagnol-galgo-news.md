From Heaven to Hell – The Story of the Galgo Espagnol
Written by
Beryl Brennan
in
Uncategorized
1 February 2026
I’ve been involved in the plight of the Spanish galgo and podenco for 18 years, publicising their plight long before the advent of facebook in 2011.  Not many people outside of Spain knew of the suffering of these wonderful hounds at the hands of the Spanish galguero and podenquero.  Galgos hung from olive trees known as The Piano (this is a rarety now), podencos kept hidden in valleys locked away, let out on hunting days, often starving and dehydrated.
After travelling regularly 6 or 7 times a month with a French rescue association, I witnessed their trauma and suffering and wrote my definitive guide to the plight of the galgo. Print copies are now all sold out but my book is available to buy as an ebook, with all proceeds of sale being donated to appeals for help with vet fees.  Please buy, and share.
