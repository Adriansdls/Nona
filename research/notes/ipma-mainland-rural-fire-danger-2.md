summary: 'IPMA Mainland Rural Fire Danger (PIR) system: real-time county-level fire
  risk classification for continental Portugal. Danger classes include VERY HIGH and
  HIGHEST, which trigger legal restrictions on burning, machinery use, and access
  in rural territories. Restrictions apply per county (conselho), selectable by district.
  Between sunset and 11:00, exceptions exist for agricultural/forestry machinery even
  at highest danger levels. Critical for Algarve territorial intelligence: fire seasons
  create search-and-rescue hazard zones with legally restricted access.'
