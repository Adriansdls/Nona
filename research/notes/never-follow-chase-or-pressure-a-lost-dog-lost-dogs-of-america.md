NEVER Follow, Chase or Pressure a Lost Dog | Lost Dogs of America
Based on thousands and thousands of cases, our three main causes of death for lost dogs are 1. Hit by a car 2. Hit by a train and 3. Drowning (often falling through thin ice).
Lost dogs who aren’t being pursued or pressured will make very wise decisions and may survive indefinitely. Lost dogs that are being pressured will stay in “panic” mode and will make very poor decisions.
Help us to help others by spreading the word. NEVER follow, chase or pressure a lost dog. Instead, allow them to relax and settle in one area. You will have a much better chance of capturing them safely.
