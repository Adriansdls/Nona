How consult statistics on road accidents?
The National Road Safety Authority makes available, through an annually prepared report, a set of information on road accidents in Portugal.
The document privileges statistical data on:
accidents with victims (minor injuries, serious injuries, deaths)
vehicles involved in accidents
accident according to the type of road
accident per district/region;
control of drink-driving.
