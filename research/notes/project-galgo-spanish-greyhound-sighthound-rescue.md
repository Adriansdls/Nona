PROJECT GALGO
Spanish Greyhound, Podenco and other Sighthound Rescue
Spanish Greyhounds (known as Galgos) and Podencos often suffer terrible lives at the hands of their hunting owners.
It has been said that the dog is man’s best friend. If this is true, then the Galgos of Spain have been viciously betrayed. There is a very cruel and barbaric custom amongst many Spanish hunters, or “Galgueros,” that destroys more than 50,000 Galgos every year.
Galgos and Podencos are used for hunting, but most are used for only one season and then discarded. If they hunt poorly, they are tortured as retribution for the shame they reflected upon their owners.
