Some lost pet recovery cases involve different resources using various techniques. The story of Potsy, an elusive Golden Retriever who escaped from his guardians, demonstrates that even when faced with challenges like deceptive hot boulders, fresh cow manure, dead drone batteries, and scammers, sometimes a helpful neighbor will be the key to reuniting a dog with his family!
Thanks for Jim Thompson with Chesapeake Thermal Drone Recovery for sharing this entertaining, back-and-forth story about Posty.
On May 9th, while visiting Narvon, Pennsylvania, a couple from West Virginia lost their dog Posty (named after Post Malone) after he got scared and took off. His humans, Kurt and Meghan, were devastated and searching tirelessly for their boy. Its bad enough when your dog go missing from your home, much worse when he is lost in another state!
Posty
On May 10th, they reached out to us (Jim & Ashley from
Chesapeake Thermal Drone Recovery
) for help. That very night, we met up and began searching. For three long hours Jim flew the thermal drone low and slow through the darkness, hoping to catch even the faintest sign of Posty. While Jim was flying, volunteer Ashley was out flyering neighborhoods, physically searching, posting on social media, networking with volunteers, and connecting with community members to spread the word as far and wide as possible.
Nothing.
But nobody stopped working. Flyers went up everywhere, the Amish community was alerted, neighbors spread the word, and an incredible network of people came together to help bring Posty home.
Then came a heartbreaking setback.
The next day, Kurt and Meghan became the victims of a cruel scam. Someone used AI to generate a fake photo of Posty lying on a table at a vet’s office. Desperate and hopeful, Kurt paid their asking price and rushed there… only to find out Posty had never been there at all. The emotional toll was unimaginable.
Scams in the lost pet industry are common. It is despicable that heartless and money-hungry scammers would take advantage of desperate pet lovers in their time of crisis, but it happens.
Still, nobody gave up.
On May 16th, we headed back out to search again. This time, local volunteer and trapper Carole joined the effort, setting trail cameras and creating an irresistible scent trail of liquid smoke, BBQ sauce, and hot dogs.
That night was one for the books.
We created a home base near a cow field. Questionable choice in retrospect. They watched and mooed with curiosity. Carole noticed one had a bag stuck in its mouth so, in true fashion, Carole army crawls under the electric fence to help the cow
Cow was saved. Woo!
Near the end of the drone flight, Jim spotted a MASSIVE heat signature. Excitement skyrocketed. Kurt and Meghan rushed off to investigate while the drone limped home on low battery… before landing into a freshly manured field. Naturally, Jim took off sprinting through the darkness to retrieve it.
At that exact moment, local resident Bob — who does human search and rescue — launched his own drone overhead to light the way. For one very confusing second, standing alone in a manure field with a spotlight shining down from the sky, Jim genuinely questioned whether he was being abducted by aliens!
Drone recovered.
A few minutes later, Kurt and Meghan returned to home base to report on that “huge heat signature.”
It was a boulder.
A very warm, very convincing boulder.
As the days passed, we discussed bringing in Carmen from
Professional Pet Trackers
to assist by using her trained tracking dogs. Plans were made for the pet tracking search dogs to come out May 22nd.
But Posty apparently had his own timeline.
At 8:20 PM on May 21st, Kurt received a call about a possible sighting. Hope returned immediately. We all prepared to begin tracking first thing the next morning.
At 7:18 AM, Jim texted the group, “Good vibes today guys!”
Two minutes later at 7:20 AM, as if he received Jim’s prophetic group text message, Posty decided he was done being lost! He casually tried to sneak into someone’s house. A kind neighbor named Daniel previously saw one of the Posty flyers and immediately recognized him. He safely secured Posty in his garage, called Kurt right away, and provided fresh food, water, and photo proof to reassure Kurt and Meghan that their boy was finally safe.
Jim & Ashley from Chesapeake Thermal Drone Recover with Posty
What an adventure Posty had! He was filthy, exhausted, unbelievably happy, and VERY ready to party. When Kurt and Meghan finally arrived, Posty exploded into whimpers, wiggles, and tail wags. The reunion was everything everyone had hoped for!
A very happy Kurt & Meghan reunited with their dog Posty
This reunion would not have happened without teamwork, networking, community support, volunteers, neighbors, and people who genuinely cared. Cases like this remind us how powerful human connection can be.
Welcome home, Posty!
