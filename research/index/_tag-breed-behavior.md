Breed explains only 9% of behavioral variation across 18,385 survey responses and 2,155 DNA-sequenced dogs. 11 genomic regions are significantly associated with behavior but none are breed-specific; most breed-stereotyped behaviors predate the origin of modern breeds (~150 years ago, Victorian era). Agnostic threshold (aggression provocation) shows no genetic breed-level signal. Emma Grigg (UC Davis): 'Breed can certainly play a role in terms of predisposing a dog to certain types of behaviors.
