Key lost-dog insights: (1) FREEZE response is characteristic in greyhounds — 'frozen or stiff posture, pupils dilated, trembling, tense closed mouth, ears back, tail tucked, body hunched, all feet stuck to floor, leaning backwards' — this explains why a lost greyhound may be visible but unresponsive.
