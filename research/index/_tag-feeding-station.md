Core protocol: place highly aromatic food (hot dogs, canned dog food, Vienna sausages, chicken) on the ground at last-sighted location; enhance with liquid smoke (hickory/mesquite) sprayed on ground, tree trunks, and nearby vegetation to extend scent radius. Critical rule: feeding station MUST be monitored with a trail camera (trail cam or Ring/Nest equivalent) — without knowing who ate the food, the search is prolonged. Camera time-stamp reveals the dog's feeding pattern for scheduling catch attempts. Wildlife competition (raccoons, cats, skunks, opossums) is unavoidable — food priority over wildlife avoidance.
